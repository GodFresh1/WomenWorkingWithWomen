Hello wonderful women of God! We are pleased that you are interested in attending the 2016 Women Working With Women Conference. The information provided below will give you an idea of what to expect during the conference and hopefully answer most of your questions.


When, Where, and Why?

When: July 15 2:00PM- July 17, 2016 2:00PM

Where: The Hilton University Of Florida Conference Center

1714 SW 34th Street, Gainesville FL, 32307

Why: As women sometimes we tend to get lost in all of the roles we play as mothers, daughters, wives, students, career women, and everything else in between. This conference will provide all of us with the tools to take our lives back, get our life on track, and live our best lives with the Lord.


What Should I Expect?

The 2016 Women Working With Women Conference is designed to be a get-away weekend for women of all ages. This is your time to relax, fellowship with other women, and most importantly, reconnect with our Heavenly Father.

The conference will cater to three types of women: women, pastor’s wives, and teens (ages 13-17). We have inspiring, spirit-filled, workshops, general sessions, and praise and worship planned for you. We will have a vendors exhibit, prizes, a banquet fit for a queen, and other activities that will have you feeling relaxed, rejuvenated, and on fire for God.


What Should I Bring?

• Bring what you would normally bring for a 3 day get away weekend.

We do plan to have a banquet on Saturday night as well as a fashion show. The banquet is formal attire. If you would like to participate in the fashion show, please indicate your interest on the registration form when you register through our Eventbrite system. (The registration and payment link are provided below in the “How to Register” section)

• Formal banquet attire

• Don't forget your camera!

• Your best attitude and an open mind =)


Where Should I Stay?

It is important to note that lodging is not included in your registration cost. Please contact the Hilton University of Florida Conference Center Hotel to reserve a room for your stay during the conference. We highly recommend staying in this hotel because it is the host site for the conference. We have blocked off rooms specifically for attendees and vendors of the conference on a first come first serve basis. Please contact them at 352-371-3600 to make your room reservation. Hotel room fees include breakfast coupons for Friday and Saturday and free Wi-Fi.


How Do I Register?

We are so glad you asked. To register please click on the registration link below. This link will take you through our Eventbrite registration process. Please select either a general women, pastor’s wife, of teen ticket so that we can place you in the appropriate workshops and seminars.

Please complete the form and have your payment ready. You will not be able to save your registration and pay later. If you are paying for more than one person at one time, please complete a registration form for each attendee. You will only have 10 minutes to complete your registration so please have all of the necessary information ready. All registration costs are subject to a service fee. The information you need to know before hand for each attendee is:

  • • First and last name
  • • Email address
  • • Home/cell phone
  • • Age
  • • If they want to participate in the fashion show
